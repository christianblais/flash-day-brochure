Competition

Flash Day is a one-day student competition with one challenge: build and ecommerce platform that can handle storms of customers without breaking a sweat (ok, maybe a little).

This event is brought to you by a cool bunch of people at Shopify.

You should come

Here's why

You want to learn about some cool programming shit
You want to learn about even more programming stuff
You enjoy working at scale. Like building HUGE piles of Red Bulls

Rules

  1. Proof of full-time enrollment in a college or university is required.
  2. Your team must have up to 4 members. Pro-tip: you'll like to have some front-end and design skills.
  3. The platform and code must be built and written on-site and on the competition day, within the allocated time.
  4. Any external libraries must be approved by the team.
  5. You need to respect the deadlines throughout the competition.
  6. Bring your own laptop and charger. We take care of the rest.

Only 8 teams can compete.

Each team application will be reviewed by members of the jury before being officially registered to the Flash Day competition.
